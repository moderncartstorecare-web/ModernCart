About ModernCart

A small store, run properly.

ModernCart Store started with a simple frustration: most charging cables, smartwatches, and home decor online look nothing like what shows up at the door. So we built a shop where you can actually rotate the product in 3D before you buy it — no surprises, no guesswork.

The store is run by Vishal Kashyap, based in Delhi, with a focus on three categories done well rather than everything done averagely: charging cables that survive daily use, smartwatches that don't feel like a compromise, and home decor pieces that are made to be looked at twice.
