About ModernCart

An intentional store, run properly.

Our Story

ModernCart started with a simple, everyday frustration: ordering products online often feels like a gamble. Most items look flawless in edited photos, but look completely different when they finally show up at your front door. We believe shopping online should be completely transparent. So, we built a digital storefront where you don't have to guess. By integrating interactive 3D modeling directly into our collections, you can completely rotate, inspect, and evaluate every trending piece from every angle before you buy it. No surprises, no guesswork—just exactly what you expected.

The store is personally run by Vishal Kashyap out of Delhi. Instead of trying to sell thousands of generic items averagely, ModernCart focuses entirely on sourcing a highly curated selection of products done exceptionally well. We track what's relevant right now, thoroughly testing every single item to ensure it balances striking design with genuine, long-lasting durability.

How it works

Email hello@moderncart.store with your order details and the reason for the return. Once it's approved, pack the item back in its original packaging — we'll arrange a pickup where available, or share a drop-off address.

Refunds go back to the original payment method within 5–7 business days of the item passing a quick quality check. Anything that arrives damaged or faulty is replaced at no extra cost.

What isn't covered

Used incense, opened cable multi-packs missing pieces, and normal wear from daily use aren't eligible for return — everything else, we've got you covered.
