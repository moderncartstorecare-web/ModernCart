About ModernCart

An intentional store, run properly.

ModernCart started with a simple, everyday frustration: ordering products online often feels like a gamble. Most items look flawless in edited photos, but look completely different when they finally show up at your front door. We believe shopping online should be completely transparent.

The store is personally run by Vishal Kashyap out of Delhi. Instead of trying to sell thousands of generic items averagely, ModernCart focuses entirely on sourcing a highly curated selection of products done exceptionally well.
